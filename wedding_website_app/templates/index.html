Our Story

We met each other in a laser tag game in San Jose. After the game, a group of people went for dessert. That’s when we had our first conversation and left each other a good impression. We started messaging each other and finally Shu-Hao asked Peggy for their first date. Soon after a few dates, Shu-Hao and Peggy found out they shared many positive attitudes toward life and officially became a couple.

Following days are full of joys. We shared everything together and explored many new things in our life. Every weekend..., new memories added up from Denver, Seattle, Hawaii...etc, where our love toward each other grew.

However, in 2019, Peggy had to leave the US because of her visa issue. We maintained a long-distance relationship in Taiwan and California. Despite the time difference, we still talked to each other every single day. Not only the love between us didn’t dwindle, we gained more trust toward each other and our relationship had since become indestructible.

In September 2019, Shu-Hao made a surprising move when Peggy came to California with her family. In a Friday evening, Shu-Hao hosted an event with a small group of friends. For Peggy, it was supposed to be an ordinary escaped-room game. Instead, it was a detailed scheme which awaited outside the exit door. Video, flowers, decorations, friends and wishes all surrounded Peggy. Shu-Hao showed up to ask Peggy to be his lovely wife and she said Yes!
